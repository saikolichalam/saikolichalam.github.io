01About

System Overview

I'm an embedded systems engineer with hands-on experience designing and deploying firmware for production-grade hardware — from medical neurostimulator devices at Nevro Corp to open-source research prototypes. I bridge the gap between hardware schematics and application software.

My expertise spans bare-metal firmware development on ARM Cortex-M platforms, real-time operating systems (FreeRTOS), multi-protocol communication stacks, PCB bring-up, and embedded network security assessment.

Currently pursuing an M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rowan University, where I also conduct research on smart materials and IoT security vulnerabilities. I'm actively seeking embedded/firmware/IoT security engineering roles.
